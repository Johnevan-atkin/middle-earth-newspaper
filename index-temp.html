The Shire Gazette

Bilbo Baggins Returns from Dragon Quest

After a year-long absence, Mr. Bilbo Baggins of Bag End has returned laden with treasure and tales of dwarves, elves, and a certain fire-breathing dragon. Neighbors report he seems... changed.

Rumors of a Mysterious Ring

Whispers from the Green Dragon suggest Bilbo carries a small golden ring of curious origin. Some say it was found deep under the Misty Mountains...

Gandalf Spotted at Bag End

The Grey Wizard was seen entering Bilbo's home late last night. What business brings him to the Shire?

New Pipe-weed in Longbottom

Farmers report a particularly fine crop this year—perfect for long evenings by the fire.

Party Field Preparations Underway

The annual harvest festival approaches. Expect fireworks, dancing, and second breakfasts.
